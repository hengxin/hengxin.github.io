About me

I received the B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science and technology from Nanjing University, Nanjing, in 2009 and 2016, respectively.

From 2017-01 to 2020-07, I was an assistant professor with the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University.

My research interests include distributed computing and formal methods. <<more>>
