About me

I am currently an associate professor in the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering at Hunan University.

I received my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University in 2009 and 2016, respectively.

From January 2017 to August 2020, I was a research assistant in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University. From September 2020 to July 2025, I was a research assistant in Software Institute at Nanjing University.

My research interests include Database Systems, Distributed Computing, and Formal Methods.   <<more>>
